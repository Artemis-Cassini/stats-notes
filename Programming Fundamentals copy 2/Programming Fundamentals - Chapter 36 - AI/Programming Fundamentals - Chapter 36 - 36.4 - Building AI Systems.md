### Building Basic AI Systems
- An **AI system** incorporates one or more AI models as system components
---
### Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- The **retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)** technique builds an AI system by supplying an AI model with external resources to improve the model's performance on specific tasks
---
### Agentic AI
- An **AI agent** is an AI system that can reason, make autonomous decisions, or take proactive actions to achieve a goal
