**Break Statements**
- A **break** statement in a loop causes the loop to exit immediately. A break statement can sometimes yield a loop that is easier to understand 

**Continue Statements**
- A **continue** statement in a loop causes an immediate jump to the while or for loop header statement. A continue statement can improve the readability of a loop.
