### Learning Objectives
- Understand how measures of center and spread are used to describe characteristics of real-life samples of data
- Understand when it is appropriate to use the mean and the  standard deviation and when it is better to use the median and the interquartile range.
- Understand the mean as the balancing point of the distribution of a sample of data and the median as the point that has roughly 50% of the distribution below it.
- Be able to write comparisons between samples of data in context.
