### Learning Objectives
- Understand that a distribution of a sample of data displays a variable’s values and the frequencies (or relative frequencies) of those values
- Know how to make graphs of distributions of numerical and categorical variables and how to interpret the graphs in context.
- Be able to compare centers and spreads of distributions of samples informally
- Pose statistical questions for numerical and categorical variables.
