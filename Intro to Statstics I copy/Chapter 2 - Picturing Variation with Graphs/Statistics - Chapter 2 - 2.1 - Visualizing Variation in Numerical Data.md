- The **distribution of a sample** of data is simply a way of organizing the data
- **Frequency** is another word for the *count* of how many times the value occurred in the collection of data

### Dotplots
- In constructing a **dotplot**, we simply put a dog above a number line where each value occurs

### Relative Frequency Histrograms
- A **relative frequency** is simply a proportion

### Stemplots
- To make a **stemplot**, divide each observation into a "stem" and a "leaf"
- The **leaf** is the last digit in the observation
- The **stem** contains all the digits that precede the leaf
