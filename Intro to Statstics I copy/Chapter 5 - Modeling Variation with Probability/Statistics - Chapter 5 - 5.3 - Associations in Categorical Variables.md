### Conditional Probabilities 
- Probabilities such as these, where we focus on just one group of objects and imagine taking a random sample from that group alone, are called **conditional probabilities** 

### Independent and Dependent Events
- Another way of putting stuff is to say that event A and event B are **associated**
- We call variables or events that are *not* associated **independent events**
