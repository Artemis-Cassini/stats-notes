### Using Simulations to Understand the Behavior of Estimators
- The probability distribution of p̂ has a special name: **sampling distribution** 
- When the bias is 0, we say that the estimator is **unbiased**
- **Precision** if reflected in the spread of the sampling distribution and is measured by using the standard deviation of the sampling distribution
- The **standard error (SE)** measures how much our estimator typically varies from sample to sample
