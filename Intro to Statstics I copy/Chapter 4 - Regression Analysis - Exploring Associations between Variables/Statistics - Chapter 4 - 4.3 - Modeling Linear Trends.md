### The Regression Line
- The **regression line** is a tool for making predictions about future observed values
- Straight lines contain a **y-intercept** and a **slope**

### Interpreting the Regression Line
- For this reason, the x-variable is called the **explanatory variable**, the **predictor variable**, or the **independent variable**
- The y-variable is called the **response variable**, the **predicted variable**, or the **dependent variable**
