### Key Terms
- Probability Distribution
- Probability Distribution Function (pdf)
- Probability Model
- Discrete Outcomes
- Continuous Outcomes
- Probability Density Curve
- Normal Curve
- Normal Distribution
- Mean of a Probability Distribution, μ
- Standard Deviation of a Probability Distribution, σ
- Normal Model: Notation, N(μ, σ)
- Standard Normal Model Notation, N(0, 1)
- Percentile
- Binomial μ Model Notation, b(n, p, x)
- n is the number of trials
- p is the probability of success on one trial
- x is the number of successes
- Cumulative Probabilities 
- Expected Values

### Learning Objectives
- Be able to distinguish between discrete and continuous variables
- Know when a Normal model is appropriate and be able to apply the model to find probabilities 
- Know when the binomial model is appropriate and be able to apply the model to find probabilities
