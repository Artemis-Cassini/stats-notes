1. The 5 main ways to prove two lines are parallel include
- AIA are congruent
- AEA are congruent
- CA are congruent
- SSIA are supp.
- SSEA are supp.

2. Two other ways to prove that two lines are parallel are
- If two lines are parallel to a third line then they are parallel to each other
- If two coplanar lines are perpendicular to the same transversal, then the two lines are parallel to each other
