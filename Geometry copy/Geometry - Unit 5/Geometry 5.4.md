1. Def. of an Isosceles Triangle - A triangle is an isosceles triangle *iff* it has two congruent sides
2. **Isosceles Triangle Thm.** If two sides of a triangle are congruent, then the base angles opposite those two sides are congruent.
3. **Converse of the Isosceles Triangle Thm.** - If two angles of a triangle are congruent, then the sides opposite those two angles are congruent
