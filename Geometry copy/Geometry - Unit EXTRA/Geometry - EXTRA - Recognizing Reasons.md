1. Def. of congruent segments - congruent segments are segments that have the same length 
2. Def. of congruent angles - congruent angles are angles that have equal measures
3. Vert. angles are congruent - vertical angles are opposite angles that are located directly across from each other on a pair of intersecting lines 
4. Def. of adjacent angles - adjacent angles like in the same plane, have a common vertex, share a common side, and do not overlap
5. Segment + Post. - if we are given segment AB and segment BC on a line, then AB + BC = AC
6. Angle + Post. - if we are given angle APB and angle BPC on a line, then the measure of angle APB + the measure of angle BPC = the measure of angle APC
7. Linear Pair Post. - if two angles form a linear pair, then the sum of their measures is 180 degrees
8. Def. of Midpt. - a midpoint is a point that divides a segment into two congruent segments
