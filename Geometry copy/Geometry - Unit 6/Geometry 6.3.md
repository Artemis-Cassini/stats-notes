1. Centroid - The point at which all three medians intersect as point G
2. The distance from a vertex to the centroid is 2 times the distance from the centroid to the midpoint of the opposite side
3. The distance from a vertex to the centroid is 2/3 times the distance from the vertex to the midpoint of the opposite side
4. The centroid is often labeled as point G because the centroid is the "center of gravity"
