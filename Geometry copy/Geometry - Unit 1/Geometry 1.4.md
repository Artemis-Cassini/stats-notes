1. A convex polygon is a polygon such that no line containing a side of the polygon contains a point in the interior of the polygon

**Number of Sides Corresponds to Name of Polygon** 
- Triangle - 3
- Quadrilateral - 4
- Pentagon - 5
- Hexagon - 6
- Heptagon - 7
- Octogon - 8
- Nonagon - 9
- Decagon - 10
- Undecagon - 11
- Dodecagon - 12
